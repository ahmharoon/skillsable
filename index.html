PTE Preperation

PTE Academic measures your English speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills in a single, short test. No more waiting around for your speaking exam – PTE Academic measures English speaking, listening, reading, and writing in just two hours.
NAATI Prepration

NAATI CCL Test determines a candidate’s ability to convey the meaning of a conversation from English language to LOTE (Language Other Than English) and vice versa. On passing CCL Test, a candidate is rewarded five bonus points also known as Credentialed Community Language.
IELTS Prepration

TIELTS Academic – is designed to measure your English language proficiency and to assess if it is suitable for an academic environment. The test reflects aspects of academic language and evaluates whether you're ready to begin training or studying in English.
