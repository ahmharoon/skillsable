ABOUT PTE COURSE

PTE, or the pearson test of English, is an academic English language exam that is computer-based and geared toward non-native English speakers who want to pursue studies overseas. It evaluates candidates’ reading, writing, speaking, and listening abilities. Questions frequently combine two skills for testing, such as speaking and reading or listening and reading.
