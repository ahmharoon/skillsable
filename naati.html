ABOUT NAATI-CCL COURSE

The setting, testing, maintaining, and certifying of translators and interpreters in Australia is the responsibility of the national accreditation authority for translators and interpreters Ltd, or NAATI. A certification that evaluates your language proficiency at the community level is the CCL (credentialed community language) test. The CCL test measures a person’s capacity to translate conversations from english to other language. Numerous dutch, Filipino, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Thai, Russian, Tamil, Persian, Sinhalese, Polish, and Nepali languages are available for CCL testing through NAATI.
